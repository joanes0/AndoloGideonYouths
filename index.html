Who We Are

Andolo Gideon Youths is a grassroots movement born in Kibera, Nairobi. We unlock potential through skill-building, environmental action, and community-led solutions. Our work is fueled by resilience, hope, and the unstoppable energy of young change-makers.

🌟 Our Vision

A self-reliant generation of young leaders transforming Kibera and beyond through innovation, equity, and sustainable development.

🎯 Our Mission

Mobilize resources, mentorship, and tools to equip youths with entrepreneurial skills, drive social impact, and build resilient communities from Kibera to the world.
